Supported formats (read and write):
  geojson    .geojson .json
  topojson   .topojson
  shapefile  .shp (+ .shx .dbf .prj)
  flatgeobuf .fgb
  kml        .kml
  kmz        .kmz
  gpx        .gpx
  wkt        .wkt
  wkb        .wkb
  csv        .csv
  png        .png (write-only; use --bbox and --size)
